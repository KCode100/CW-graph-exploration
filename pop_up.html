POP UP PANEL

This pop up panel will have various additional features/views which will allow powerful data analysis such as:

* regional map (with commonwealth countries indicated)

* ability to filter - by region (activated here), by commonwealth/non-commonwealth, by country

* ability to compare - region v region, country v country, commonwealth v non-commonwealth

* ability to drill down into datasets which drive categories - ie Enrolment in Secondary Education, Literacy Rate, Digital Natives for EDUCATION

* view top 10, bottom 10, risers/fallers (choosing your own category or dataset to compare)

* show min, max, mean, global mean, regional mean on all charts
